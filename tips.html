10 Essential Questions to Ask Before Renting a Wedding Tent

Planning Guide June 15, 2023 8 min read

Renting a wedding tent is one of the most important decisions you'll make when planning an outdoor wedding. The right tent can transform any space into a magical venue, while the wrong choice could lead to weather disasters or guest discomfort. To ensure you get exactly what you need, here are the 10 most important questions to ask tent rental companies.

1. What size tent do we need for our guest count?

This is the most fundamental question. A good rule of thumb is to allow 15-20 square feet per guest for a seated dinner with dancing. However, this varies based on your table arrangement, dance floor size, and whether you're including a buffet or bar stations inside the tent. Always provide your rental company with your exact guest count and layout preferences.

2. What types of tents do you offer?

Common options include:

  • Frame tents: No center poles, good for all surfaces
  • Pole tents: Classic look but require staking into grass
  • Clear span tents: Large column-free spaces
  • Stretch tents: Modern, flexible fabric structures
Ask to see photos of each style and discuss which would work best for your venue and aesthetic.

3. What's included in the rental price?

Some companies offer all-inclusive packages while others charge separately for each component. Clarify what's included regarding:

  • Sidewalls (clear or solid)
  • Flooring options
  • Lighting packages
  • Heating or cooling systems
  • Delivery, setup, and takedown fees
Get a detailed written quote to avoid surprises.

Pro Tip: Always ask about the weight capacity if you're planning to hang heavy decor like chandeliers or floral installations from the tent structure.

4. What are your weather contingency plans?

Outdoor weddings need backup plans for rain, wind, or extreme temperatures. Ask:

  • How quickly can sidewalls be added if rain appears?
  • What wind speed can the tent withstand?
  • Do you offer heating solutions for cool evenings?
  • What about cooling options for summer weddings?
Reputable companies will have solutions for all weather scenarios.

5. Do we need any permits?

Many locations require permits for large tents, especially if they're being installed in public parks or historic properties. Your rental company should know local regulations regarding:

  • Fire safety requirements
  • Maximum occupancy limits
  • Noise ordinances
  • Parking considerations
Factor in permit processing times which can take weeks in some areas.

Quick Checklist: Tent Rental Must-Ask Questions

  • Size requirements for guest count
  • Available tent styles and materials
  • Complete pricing breakdown
  • Weather protection options
  • Permit requirements

Asking these essential questions will help you find the perfect wedding tent rental company and ensure your outdoor celebration goes smoothly, no matter what nature has in store. Remember that quality tent companies will welcome these questions and have detailed answers ready - if they seem vague or dismissive, consider it a red flag.

Transform Your Wedding Tent: 15 Stunning Decoration Ideas

Decor Ideas May 22, 2023 6 min read

A wedding tent provides a blank canvas for creating your dream wedding aesthetic. With the right decorations, you can transform a simple tent into an enchanting space that reflects your personal style. Here are 15 inspiring ideas to elevate your wedding tent decor from ordinary to extraordinary.

1. Draped Ceilings with Fabric

Soft billows of chiffon, tulle, or satin fabric create a romantic canopy effect. Choose fabrics in your wedding colors and consider adding twinkle lights woven through the draping for a magical evening glow. This works especially well with high-peaked tents.

2. Hanging Floral Installations

Suspended floral arrangements make a dramatic statement. Options include:

  • Floral chandeliers over dining tables
  • Garlands running the length of the tent
  • Individual hanging blooms at varying heights
Work with your florist to select flowers that will stay fresh throughout your event.

3. Statement Lighting

Lighting sets the mood. Consider mixing:

  • Vintage chandeliers for elegance
  • Edison bulb strings for rustic charm
  • LED uplighting to wash tent walls in color
  • Paper lanterns for a playful touch
Dimmers allow you to adjust lighting as the evening progresses.

Pro Tip: Always check weight limits before hanging heavy decor from tent frames. Your rental company can advise on safe installation methods.

These are just a few of the countless ways to personalize your wedding tent. The key is choosing elements that reflect your personality as a couple while creating a cohesive look throughout the space. With thoughtful planning, your tent will become more than just a shelter—it will be an unforgettable part of your wedding story.

The Complete Guide to Wedding Tent Flooring Options

Setup Tips April 10, 2023 10 min read

One of the most overlooked yet crucial aspects of wedding tent setup is flooring. The right flooring can enhance comfort, safety, and aesthetics while the wrong choice could lead to tripping hazards or ruined heels. This guide breaks down all the options to help you make the best decision for your venue and budget.

1. Natural Ground (Grass/Dirt)

Pros:

  • Most affordable option (often free)
  • Natural, rustic look
  • Easy setup
Cons:
  • Uneven surface can be uncomfortable
  • Heels sink into grass
  • Muddy if it rains
  • Difficult for wheelchair access
Best for: Casual daytime events in dry weather with flat ground.

2. Wood Flooring

Wood decking creates an elegant, elevated surface. Options include:

  • Hardwood: Beautiful but expensive
  • Engineered wood: More affordable alternative
  • Plywood: Budget option that can be covered with carpet
Always ensure proper support beams are installed beneath to prevent flexing or bouncing when dancing.

3. Carpet Flooring

Carpet adds warmth and comfort underfoot. Considerations:

  • Choose commercial-grade carpet for durability
  • Patterned carpets hide stains better
  • Requires padding underneath for comfort
  • Can be laid over wood or plywood subfloor
Many rental companies offer carpet in various colors to match your theme.

Flooring Decision Factors

  • Budget: Costs range from $0 (grass) to $15+ per square foot (hardwood)
  • Weather: Wet conditions demand non-slip surfaces
  • Accessibility: Smooth surfaces needed for wheelchairs
  • Guest Comfort: Consider hours of standing/dancing

Your wedding tent flooring should balance practicality with aesthetics. While beautiful hardwood might be ideal, your budget or venue terrain might make another option more sensible. Discuss all possibilities with your tent rental company early in the planning process, as flooring choices can affect tent sizing and setup requirements.
